About Us


MyCoNet is a community project of Boulder Biomimicry that promotes collaboration and community engagement of all forms in Boulder and beyond. In addition to developing this online platform, we host monthly collaboration workshops the first Saturday of every month at Alfalfa’s Boulder from 4-8 PM. We are also engaged in facilitating collaborative community projects. We are partnered with the Permaculture Action Network and Solutions Voyage to bring collaboration and direct action to music events in the Denver and Boulder area.


We exemplify collaboration by synergizing our diverse talents to advance our mutual goal of increasing ecosocial cohesion. Our core team has passionately executed several projects over the past two years and have cultivated the concept of MyCoNet since its inception over one year ago. We are currently seeking web developers willing to work for equity.
